About Career Avatars

Career Avatars, an organization founded by Jaspreet Kaur follows a scientific and standardized process to evaluate a client’s aptitude and personality. This process helps us to obtain the most important information about a client’s behaviour, emotional strength, interests and skills. With the received information we facilitate on what kind of careers would be suitable for them. We aim to provide the following services:

• Career psychometric and aptitude assessment
• Student profile evaluation
• Extensive analysis across many career paths
• Customized counselling sessions for 8-12th grade students, college graduate, working professional
• Various national/international universities admissions and visa filing
• Dedicated assessments from all skill levels
• Complete Guidance with a back-up plan, etc

We provide the resources and knowledge that a career expert possesses. Career Avatars is the best-suited platform for students to explore various career avenues. Our experts will assess your aptitude, personality, interests and your approach towards life. We use tools and insights that will make a difference in your career, business and personal life.

About Jaspreet Kaur

Jaspreet is backed by a Bachelor of Commerce (B. Com) in International Business from St. Francis Degree College (Hyderabad), Master of Business Administration (MBA) in Human Resources & Finance from Vignana Jyothi Institute of Management (Hyderabad). She holds a Certification in Global Career Counsellor (GCC) green belt in Career counselling from Univariety, Singapore and the University of California, Los Angeles (UCLA Extension). Jaspreet has 5 years of corporate experience in finance and recruitment in reputed MNCs. Apart from Career Avatar, she is also contributing to a wellness program to motivate people to live a happy and healthy lifestyle.

An independent thinker, Jaspreet believes as a career counsellor every student comes across a stage in life where he/she knows what they like or want, but have difficulty envisaging it. In her own words, her only reason to be a career counsellor was to understand students and their needs better and act as a lighthouse to help them navigate in their career path.
